About Me

I’m happy to share that I’ve achieved a BA with Distinction in Graphic Design.

Art & design has always been my main passion; it goes back as far as I can remember. In-fact, one of my earliest memories was a regret! For a few pennies, I sold one of my drawings to a classmate in primary school. I bought it back shortly after as it felt important to me. I’ve still got it to this day. I can now see why it was important to me. It was the first transaction that led me to where I am today, I’m proud that I am in a position doing something I love.

Being a passionate person, I understand the dedication and commitment that goes into establishing your own brand. I’m here to help visualise your ideas and bring them to life using contemporary knowledge and technique.
