Print Project

Student Project

The Brief

This brief allowed creative freedom, with the subject focused on a dinner party inviting guests from any time period. The aim was to produce a physical artefact showcasing typographic skill.

Content

I used a radial system for my expressive type, featuring six eccentric figures, from an odd documentarian to a cult leader. To reflect their lunacy, I staggered the radial effect on the body copy. The type also mimics the chaos of a dinner table, with text impacted by things thrown, smashed, or spilled.

The Artefact

My artefact is a saddle-stitched self-cover with citrine paper, black text, and magenta accents, inspired by the punk movement. The expressive text with chaotic effects, mimic a rowdy dinner party argument. A 12-column grid and minor third ratio structure ensured hierarchy and readability.
