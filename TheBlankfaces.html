The Blankfaces

In house position

Logo Design

For the redesign of the acronym logo, I focused on aligning it with the brand's streetwear fashion style while also making a meaningful connection to homelessness. The new logo features hand-drawn marker typography, inspired by messages written on cardboard.

The logo is prominently displayed across the bold graphic tees and hoodies I created to build social awareness for homelessness. 

Apparel

I created a range of graphics to communicate The Blankfaces' mission effectively. Each one was designed to be impactful and politically charged to resonate with their target market.

I consolidated all of their designs into an ordered system and converted the files from a screen to DTG printing format. This allowed me to set up print-on-demand for their website sales.

Website Design

When designing The Blankfaces' website, I adopted a mobile-first approach as the web traffic called for it. This ensures that users can easily access The Blankfaces' shop from social media links and QR codes on a range of stickers I created.

The website also maintained the brand’s visual identity and social message.
